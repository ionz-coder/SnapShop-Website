About SnapShop

Welcome to SnapShop, your premier destination for official MITL merchandise and campus pride!

We are dedicated to providing high-quality, officially licensed products that allow students, alumni, and supporters to showcase their Cardinal pride in style. From comfortable shirts to collectible pins, every item is designed with the MITL community in mind.

Our mission is to strengthen campus spirit through creative and quality merchandise that every Cardinal can be proud to wear and display.
